How to play ANTHROFRACTAL:

  • Every new communication will offer you new choices.
  • You will be given 2-4 choices every communication.
  • Every new communication will tell you how long you have to make your choice.
  • Everyone playing gets the opportunity to choose. Cast your vote, and the option with the most votes will win.
  • Cast your vote on the social sites ANTHROFRACTAL updates to.
  • You can vote on multiple sites if you desire.
  • If you donate via one or more of the donation options available, you can receive more votes, and therefore more influence.
  • Your collective influence will determine the story of ANTHROFRACTAL.
